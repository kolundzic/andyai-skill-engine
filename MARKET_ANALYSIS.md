# Market Analysis v0.2

## Market Observation

The AI market is increasingly splitting into multiple layers:

1. model layer
2. infrastructure layer
3. application layer
4. orchestration / execution layer

The orchestration layer is underbuilt compared to its future importance.

## What Most Players Sell

### Model Vendors
They sell intelligence generation.

### Infra Vendors
They sell compute, latency, throughput.

### App Vendors
They sell domain workflows.

### What AndyAI Skill Engine Targets
It targets **reusable execution structure**.

## Why This Is Valuable

Enterprises do not just need answers.
They need:
- repeatability
- portability
- policy control
- reviewability
- compatibility across ecosystems

That creates room for a skill-engine category.

## Market Signal

Copilot ecosystem growth is a signal that:
- users want packaged capability
- ecosystems reward reusable integrations
- compatibility layers increase reach

But the compatibility target is not the whole business.
The deeper asset is the engine that generates those packages.

## Strategic Conclusion

AndyAI Skill Engine should be positioned as:
- a master skill architecture
- a compatibility-ready execution layer
- a future bridge between human intent and verifiable AI operations
