# Apache-2.0 Decision Note v1.0

## Decision

Use Apache-2.0 for the public core of `andyai-skill-engine`.

## Why

- standard and recognizable
- enterprise-friendly
- permissive and low-friction
- strong public credibility signal

## Tradeoff

This is not anti-cloning protection.
Commercial defensibility must come from the enterprise layer, not from the public core license itself.
