# LICENSING DECISION MATRIX v1.1

| Option | Visibility | Adoption Potential | Copy Protection | Investor Story | Best Use |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---|
| MIT / Apache-2.0 | High | High | Low | Strong for ecosystem play | Community-first core |
| Fair Source / source-available | High | Medium | Medium/High | Strong for controlled platform play | Protected public core |
| Full private | Low | Low | High | Strong only if sales motion already exists | Closed enterprise product |

## Recommended score for current stage

### Apache-2.0
Best if you want:
- broad credibility
- easy reuse
- standard open-source optics

### Fair Source
Best if you want:
- public code
- stronger anti-cloning business posture
- delayed or restricted commercial freedom

### Full private
Not recommended for current stage unless:
- you already have enterprise customers waiting
- or the repo contains your main monetization moat
